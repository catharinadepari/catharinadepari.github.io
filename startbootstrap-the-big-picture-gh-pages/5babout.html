the content of the website introduces you to key aspects influencing the volcano's sustainabilty: environment, community, and the government

Environment

Mount Merapi is the most active vocano in Indonesia, situated in the province of Yogyakarta Special Regions and Central Java. With the height of 2,968 m above sea level, the volcano is classified as one of 16 world’s volcanoes iin the United States Geological Survey’s Decade Volcanoes. The eruption in 1006 is indicated as the deadliest event which to this date, still rises controversies and debates. Some believed that the eruption had impacted the lives of ancient community of Javanese kingdom during that period.
